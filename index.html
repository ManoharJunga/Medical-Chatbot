The Rich History of Hyderabad

Hyderabad, founded in 1591 by Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah, has a rich history of Nizams and architectural marvels. The city boasts historical landmarks like Charminar, Golconda Fort, and Chowmohallah Palace.
